ABOUT THE CONFERENCE

About iCDEM 2027

The International Conference on Diamond and Emerging Materials (iCDEM 2027) brings together researchers, scientists, academicians and industry experts from around the world to discuss recent advances in diamond science, materials engineering and emerging technologies.

The conference provides an international platform for presenting research findings, exchanging innovative ideas and strengthening collaborations between academia and industry.
