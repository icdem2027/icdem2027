About iCDEM 2027

The International Conference on Diamond and Emerging Materials (iCDEM 2027) brings together leading researchers, scientists, academicians and industry experts from around the world to discuss the latest innovations in diamond science, advanced materials and emerging technologies.

The conference provides an international platform for exchanging knowledge, presenting research findings, fostering collaborations and promoting future scientific developments.
