HOW TO REACH

Travel Information

IIT Madras is well connected by Air, Rail and Road.

Chennai International Airport

Approximately 12 km from IIT Madras. Taxi services, app-based cabs and Metro connectivity are available.

Chennai Central Railway Station

Approximately 15 km from IIT Madras. Prepaid taxis, Metro Rail and city buses are available.

Local Transportation

Uber, Ola, Auto-rickshaws, MTC buses and Chennai Metro provide convenient transportation.
