Journal Submission (Coming Soon)

The Frontiers in Carbon journal special issue for iCDEM 2026 conference topic is now online

iCDEM 2026 is pleased to be partnering with Frontiers in Carbon to offer authors the opportunity to submit research presented at the conference. Frontiers in Carbon is a multidisciplinary, gold open-access journal dedicated to advancing cutting-edge research in carbon-based materials across various fields, including electronics, catalysis, composites and biotechnology.

All authors with accepted submissions (either oral or poster) will be invited to submit their work for consideration for publication in Frontiers in Carbon. Authors may revise their manuscripts after receiving feedback during the conference before final submission.
